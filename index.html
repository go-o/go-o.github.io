GO!

is what I tell myself when I make decisions. Once, I jumped off a rocky cliff 80 feet above a small pond of water. If I had hesitated, I wouldn't have jumped far enough away from the rock wall and would have died. GO! I have no idea how any of my work will compare to my peers, ever. But I can't have my feet stuck on different sides of a river! GO! My little brother (16) says I get intimidated to run when I see others far ahead of me. In the words of physics professor Brent Corbin, "Well.. *walking with hands stiffly stuffed in pockets* you'd say the mouse is 1 foot in front of you.. BUUT George is a foot behind you, and Bob yet ANOTHER foot behind him... so, well, position is relative.. to the observer, obviously." GO!
